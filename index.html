Welcome to DataGorge: NBA Stats Database

  You can navigate to the tables for the entities in our database above. They are described here:
  • Players: This contains the NBA players chosen to be in our database.
  • Teams: All teams in the NBA.
  • Statistics: Single-game statistics for individual players.
  • Games: Info for games between teams.
  • Positions: All valid NBA positions.
